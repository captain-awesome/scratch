Weekend On-Call (Saturday and Sunday): If you work more then your 8 hour shift during the weekend, your compensation will be based on the following structure listed below.  The Comp Time earned for working extra hours on weekends must be taken the following buiness day (Monday).  Use it or lose it.*

Up to 2 hours: You will receive a half-day off.
Up to 4 hours: You will receive a full day off.
Up to 8 hours: You will receive a day and a half off.
